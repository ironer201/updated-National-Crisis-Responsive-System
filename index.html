Passive Report

A passive reporting system allows victims and witnesses to anonymously report crimes like extortion, harassment, and eve-teasing, ensuring more incidents are documented.
By analyzing reports, authorities can identify crime patterns and take preventive measures. Increased accountability pushes law enforcement to act, while faster response times help reduce recurring offenses, making communities safer.

  • Enables anonymous complaints, ensuring more cases are reported.
  • Helps track crime patterns for better preventive actions.
  • Increases accountability, pushing authorities to act.
  • Improves response time, reducing recurring crimes.
